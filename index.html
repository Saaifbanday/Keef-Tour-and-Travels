Famous Foods of Kashmir

Embark on a journey to find Kashmirs famous dishes: Because a journey is only complete when your taste buds join the adventure. Indulge in Wazwan's rich flavors—Goshtaab, Yakhni, and iconic Rogan Josh. From savory Tabak Maaz to sweet Phirni to aroumatic Kehwa, each dish narrates a story, promising a sensory exploration in the heart of Kashmir's mesmerizing destination.


Indulge in Wazwan's rich flavors—Goshtaab, Yakhni, and iconic Rogan Josh. As you savor these culinary delights, allow each dish to transport you deeper into the heart of Kashmiri culture. The Goshtaab, with its tender lamb meatballs infused with a blend of fragrant spices, delivers a burst of flavors that dance on your palate. Meanwhile, the Yakhni, a delectable yogurt-based dish, boasts a creamy texture and a subtle tang that harmonizes beautifully with tender pieces of meat, creating a comforting embrace for your taste buds.
