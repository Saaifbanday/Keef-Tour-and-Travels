Skiing in Kashmir

Kashmir is a premier destination for skiing enthusiasts. The snow-covered slopes offer incredible skiing experiences.

  • Best Time: December to March
  • Rental Options: Available at Gulmarg

Heliskiing Adventures

For those seeking a thrilling adventure, heliskiing in Kashmir offers access to remote peaks and untouched powder.

  • Best Time: January to February
  • Safety First: Always hire a certified guide.

Bird Watching in Kashmir

Experience the rich bird life of Kashmir by exploring its diverse ecosystems. From the Himalayan Monal to the Black-necked Crane, Kashmir offers a paradise for bird watchers.

  • Best Locations: Hokersar, Dachigam National Park
  • Guided Tours Available
